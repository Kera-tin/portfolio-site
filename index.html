About Vincent

I’m an engineer and researcher working at the intersection of electrical systems design, control systems design, industrial automation, and supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA).

My background spans electrical services design, electrical panel design, PLC / HMI / SCADA architectures, and AI-driven prognosis and system health monitoring. I have contributed to projects of such caliber from mining operations and industrial plants to transmission grids and academic research labs.

I care about clean abstractions, strong documentation, and testable designs—whether I am writing C for a microcontroller, modeling a converter in Simulink, or design electrical systems. I thrive in teams where controls engineers, power specialists, and software developers work side-by-side to ship impactful systems.
